Who am I

My name is Miguel and I'm a queer 24 year-old designer currently based on Madrid. I studied Design, focusing on interactive design.

  • Proactive I'm a highly motivated person
  • Goal-oriented and very focused on details
  • Tidy I'm basically the Marie Kondo of UX design
  • Team player Though there is an 'I' in Miguel

What am I about

As my BA in Design was fairly generalist, my perspective on design is broader and so my core values in the matter extend any field:

  • User Empathy for me, the single most important thing in any project.
  • Facilitate pleasures in contrast to fixing problems, which I think is a given, but we're allowed to have a little fun.
  • Moral Design How can the values of the client be reflected in the logistics, sourcing and the final product.

What we're not gonna do

As an active member of the LGBTQIA+ Community, I've come into contact with a myriad of different walks of life, so the following are deal-breakers for me:

  • Whateverphobia Be it based on gender, gender identity, sexual orientation, race. We don't do that here.
  • Patronizing I'm young, but I know my stuff. Let me ask you for help if I need it
